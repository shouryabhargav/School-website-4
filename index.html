About us

Mayoor School, Noida was established in the year 2003 under the aegis of Mayo College General Council Ajmer. In these seventeen years, the school has grown in strength and blossomed from a fledgling to a bird, ready to soar to newer heights. The school building is infused with life echoing with children’s excitement and enthusiasm. The splendid school campus is spread over more than 10 acres and houses the Academic Blocks, the Administrative Block, the Amphitheatre and huge playgrounds for Football, Cricket, Horse Riding and Skating besides Tennis, Basketball and Volleyball Courts. Mayoor has an exclusive Sports Complex encampassing an indoor Swimming Pool, Shooting Range, Squash, Badminton and Aerobics. A Rock Climbing Wall has been erected to inculcate the spirit of adventure in Mayoorians. A well-stocked library, ultra modern Science Labs, Maths Lab, and Computer Labs nurture the element of inquisitiveness in each child. Smart Boards facilitate teachers in making teaching interesting and effective and the well equipped comfortable Sick Bay looks after children when they are not well. Excellent student-teacher ratio, spacious and air-conditioned classrooms and buses, yet competitive fee structure make Mayoor the most sought after destination on the expressway.. The school with a strength of more than 3200 students has been constantly treading the path of success. Outstanding results of class X and XII are a testimony to this. Our students have got admission to various reputed colleges in India and abroad. A project based Indo-German Student Exchange programme helped children get an exposure to global standards. Also, the school is once again the proud recipient of International School Award accredited by the British Council under the dynamic leadership of our Principal, Mrs Alka Awasthi who has traversed an illustrious career spanning more than 22 years. She is a visionary and a change maker. All her initiatives are driven by academic expertise and a passion to touch the lives of her students in a special way. Under her able guidance, the school is set to soar higher and achieve greater success in the years to come. The aim of Education proposes nothing less than to build the ideal society of tomorrow by planning the cultivation of human resources today. As the artist works on canvas or marble to create new beauty, we at Mayoor work on minds to shape a better world for tomorrow.

Admission Procedure



Age for Pre-Nursery, Nursery, KG as on

1st April 2020
Pre-Nursery: 2 years 3 months
Nursery: 3 years +
Kindergarten: 4 years +

Prospectus and Registration forms are available at the school reception or can be downloaded from the school website.[From "Download files" section]. After completing the formalities, parents have to submit the form at the school reception. Incomplete forms will not be shortlisted for the interaction.

Procedure for Registration

Application for Registration is attached with the Prospectus. The registration form can be downloaded from the website also. The application along with registration fee, attested copy of the child's Birth Certificate, photographs of the parents, address proof and vaccination card are to be deposited at the School Reception. The process of registration does not guarantee admission.

Interaction (For classes Pre-Nursery ,Nursery and KG)



On completion of the registration process, parents will be informed about the date of interaction. This will be an informal interaction of the student and parents with the Principal and Head Mistress. Based on the interaction and the number of vacancies in each class, parents will be notified. Once the admission is confirmed, school fees & documents required are to be submitted with in the given time.

Entrance Examination (For Classes I – IX)



On completion of the registration process, parents will be informed about the date of the written entrance test for the students of Class I onwards. This will be followed by an informal interaction of the student and parents with the Principal and Vice-Principal. Based on the test results, the interaction and the number of vacancies in each class, parents will be notified. Once the admission is confirmed, school fees and documents required are to be submitted with in the given time.
